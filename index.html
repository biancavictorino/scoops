Our Menu

Explore Our Irresistible Ice Cream Creations

We believe that every scoop should be a delightful adventure. Our menu is bursting with a variety of flavors, from timeless classics to innovative creations. Each ice cream is handcrafted with the finest ingredients to bring you pure joy in every bite. Explore our menu and find your new favorite treat!

Sweet Banana Bliss

Dive into our Banana Split Extravaganza, a delightful fusion of classic flavors. Featuring creamy banana ice cream layered with rich chocolate syrup, strawberry sauce, and whipped cream, all crowned with a cherry and crunchy chopped nuts. This timeless treat is a celebration of sweet indulgence.

Cosmic Cookie Monster

Embark on a galactic adventure with the Cosmic Cookie Monster. This vibrant blue cookie dough ice cream is packed with chunks of chocolate chip cookies and swirls of gooey fudge, delivering an out-of-this-world flavor experience that's perfect for cookie lovers of all ages.

Decadent Fudge Mountain

Indulge in our Decadent Fudge Mountain, a towering delight for chocolate lovers. A warm, gooey brownie base provides the foundation for a generous scoop of velvety vanilla ice cream, drizzled with rich chocolate sauce and crowned with a sprinkle of crunchy nuts.

Rocky Road to Paradise

Embark on a journey of pure indulgence with Rocky Road to Paradise. This decadent chocolate ice cream is loaded with fluffy marshmallows, crunchy almonds, and swirls of rich chocolate fudge. It's a rocky road you’ll want to travel again and again.

Fantasy Pancake Pillows

Indulge in our Fantasy Pancake Pillows, inspired by the traditional Japanese treat, Imagawayaki. These golden-brown pancake spheres are filled with a choice of sweet red bean paste, decadent chocolate ganache, or creamy custard

S'mores Campfire Craze

Relive your favorite campfire memories with S'mores Campfire Craze. Toasted marshmallow ice cream is mixed with graham cracker pieces and generous swirls of rich chocolate. It's a nostalgic treat that brings the cozy campfire experience straight to your taste buds.
